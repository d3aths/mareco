About Us

We are a reliable, small, sole-trader business providing high-quality maintenance services for marine, residential, and commercial properties across Nelson, Tasman and surrounding areas. Our mission is to ensure the upkeep of your property with exceptional attention to detail and a commitment to delivering results on time and within budget.

We take pride in offering tailored maintenance solutions for the unique needs of our customers. Whether you need routine servicing, urgent repairs, or specialized solutions, Mareco is here to keep your property in top condition.

Why chose Mareco?

We understand that maintenance is an ongoing necessity, not just a one-time service. With years of experience in the industry and a team focused on providing the highest quality service, here’s why you should choose Mareco:

Comprehensive Solutions: We cater to a wide variety of needs—whether for boats, homes, or commercial spaces.
Local Expertise: Proudly servicing the Nelson and Tasman regions, we understand the specific needs of the local environment.

Reliable & Trustworthy:

As a sole-trader business, our reputation is everything. We focus on building long-term relationships with our clients.

Affordable & Transparent:

We offer competitive pricing with no hidden costs. You’ll always know exactly what to expect.

Our Services

At Mareco, we offer a broad range of maintenance services designed to meet the needs of marine, residential, and commercial property owners. Our expertise covers:

Marine Maintenance Services

  • - Boat and marine equipment repairs
  • - Hull cleaning and antifouling
  • - Dockside and slipway assistance

Residential Maintenance Services

  • - Home repairs and renovations
  • - Lawn and garden maintenance
  • - Painting and decorating
  • - Section clean ups and fence work
  • - Gutter cleaning and roof maintenance, waterblasting

Commerical Property Maintenance

  • - Repairs and renovations
  • - Retail space maintenance and painting
  • - After hours upkeep and cleaning
  • - Parking lot and landscaping maintenance
